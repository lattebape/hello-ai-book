# 你好，AI —— 第一册《原来AI是这样》

**Hello, AI — Book One: *So That's What AI Is***

一本写给零基础读者的 AI 科普图文书：不写一行代码、不出现一个数学公式，只用生活比喻和手绘风矢量插图，把 AI 讲明白。全书由作者与 AI 结对创作——包括每一张插图。

An illustrated AI primer for readers with zero technical background: not a single line of code, not a single equation — just everyday analogies and storybook-style vector illustrations. Written and illustrated by the author working in tandem with an AI.

**作者 / Author：胖土匪Lecato**

## ✨ 这本书的特点 / Highlights

- **零门槛**：面向从未接触过编程和高等数学的读者，测试标准是"递给家里长辈也能读懂并会心一笑"。
- **59 张原创矢量插图**：两位向导"小悠"和"泡泡"贯穿全书，示意图全部为 SVG 矢量图，中英文各一套。
- **一条主线讲到底**：全书围绕一句话展开——"AI 从大量数据里学出规律，再用规律做预测和判断"。
- **Zero barrier to entry**: written for readers who have never coded; the quality bar is "hand it to a family member and they can follow along."
- **59 original vector illustrations**: two guides — Yoyo and Bubble — accompany the reader; every diagram ships in both Chinese and English.

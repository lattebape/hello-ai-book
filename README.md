# 你好，AI —— 零基础AI科普三部曲（全系列完结）

**Hello, AI — A Three-Book AI Primer for Absolute Beginners (Complete)**

一套写给零基础读者的 AI 科普图文书：不写一行代码、不出现一个数学公式，只用生活比喻和手绘风矢量插图，把 AI **讲明白、用起来、处得好**。全系列由作者与 AI 结对创作——包括每一张插图。

An illustrated AI trilogy for readers with zero technical background: not a single line of code, not a single equation — just everyday analogies and storybook-style vector illustrations, covering how AI **works**, how to **use** it, and how to **live** with it. Written and illustrated by the author working in tandem with an AI.

**作者 / Author：胖土匪Lecato**

## 📚 三册讲什么 / What Each Book Covers

**第一册《原来AI是这样》**——拆开看懂：AI 早就在你身边 · 七十年接力赛 · 机器怎么"学习" · 超大规模文字接龙 · 怎么"看"和"画" · 为什么一本正经地胡说八道 · 能力边界 · 高频词小词典 · 相处的正确姿势。

**Book One, *So That's What AI Is*** — how it works: AI all around you · a seventy-year relay · how machines learn · the word-chain game · seeing and painting · nonsense with a straight face · capability borders · a buzzword dictionary · how to live with it.

**第二册《把AI用起来》**——上手用好：挑一个AI说出第一句话 · 提问的四块积木 · 追问的艺术 · 生活万事屋 · 工作加速器 · 随身家教 · 创作伙伴 · 安全使用手册 · 你的AI工作流。

**Book Two, *Putting AI to Work*** — hands-on craft: first words · four building blocks of asking · the art of the follow-up · the everyday fix-it shop · the work accelerator · a tutor in your pocket · a creative partner · the safety manual · your personal AI workflow.

**第三册《与AI同行》**——想明白怎么活：工作会变成什么样 · 给孩子的AI课 · 陪爸妈跨过数字鸿沟 · 真假难辨的时代 · AI时代的饭碗 · 大家都在吵什么 · 往前看一眼 · 尾声：人的位置。

**Book Three, *Living with AI*** — living with the change: what happens to work · an AI lesson for the kids · helping parents across the digital divide · the age of hard-to-tell · earning a living · what everyone is arguing about · a glance down the road · epilogue: the human seat.

## ✨ 这套书的特点 / Highlights

- **零门槛**：面向从未接触过编程和高等数学的读者，测试标准是"递给家里长辈也能读懂并会心一笑"。
- **100+ 张原创矢量插图**：两位向导"小悠"和"泡泡"贯穿三册，示意图全部为 SVG 矢量图，中英文各一套。
- **一条主线讲到底**：第一册的每个原理，都是第二册的一个技巧、第三册的一个判断依据。
- **不贩卖焦虑、不替读者站队**：争议议题双方理由并列呈现，结论读者自己下。
- **Zero barrier to entry**: written for readers who have never coded; the quality bar is "hand it to a family member and they can follow along."
- **100+ original vector illustrations** across the trilogy; every diagram ships in both Chinese and English.
- **One thread throughout**: every principle in Book One becomes a technique in Book Two and a judgment tool in Book Three.

## 📂 仓库结构 / Repository Layout

```
ebook/                     六本完整版 PDF / all six final PDFs
book1/  book2/  book3/     各册目录 / one folder per book
  ├─ zh/                   中文书稿（Markdown）/ Chinese manuscript
  ├─ en/                   英文书稿（Markdown）/ English manuscript
  └─ illustrations/
       ├─ zh/              中文版插图（SVG）/ Chinese illustrations
       └─ en/              英文版插图变体（SVG）/ English illustration variants
INDEX.md                   文件索引（含插图原名对照）/ file index
```

无文字或仅含通用符号的配图为中英共用，存于各册 `illustrations/zh/`；`illustrations/en/` 只存含文字插图的英文变体。

Text-free artwork is shared between both language editions and lives in each book's `illustrations/zh/`; `illustrations/en/` holds the English variants of text-bearing diagrams only.
